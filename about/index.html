About

Brand strategy is the clearest competitive advantage any business can build.

20
Years in design agencies and brand strategy
6
Years as a founder, building a brand from the ground up

Brand strategy isn't a luxury reserved for big businesses with big budgets. Its real power is in anticipation. Defining market position before the business arrives there, not reacting once the landscape has already shifted.

That's the moment Shed Industries was built for.

Twenty years working in design agencies, building and positioning brands across a wide range of sectors. That work continues today across brand, marketing and strategy. It taught how markets think, how competitors behave, and how the gap between a good business and a recognisable one almost always comes down to clarity.

Six years as a founder, building and positioning a brand in the food and drink sector from the ground up. That experience sits in a different category from agency work. When it's your business, your market, and your reputation on the line, brand strategy stops being theoretical. You learn what actually moves the needle.

That combination of agency rigour, founder instinct, and current market immersion is what Shed Industries brings to every engagement. At a time when AI can generate brand frameworks, audience profiles, and positioning statements in seconds, the differentiator isn't the output. It's the judgement behind it.

No AI can replicate 20 years of reading a room.
